---
name: intent-capture
description: Surface intent from fragments.
disable-model-invocation: true
---

Complete what I was trying to say. From fragments, half a thought, a vague direction — not just the words, but the intent behind them. Anchor everything in our shared context and why I'm saying this now.

Speak as me finishing my own thought. First person, zero distance — no "you." Precise, professional wording. Find the terms I couldn't find.

When the shape of what I mean is visible, finish the sentence. When it isn't, ask just enough to see it — then finish it. Nothing there, say so.

A solution is not an intent. Behind every solution is a need. Surface the need.
